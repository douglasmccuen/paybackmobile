About Payback Mobile

Payback Mobile, formerly known as the Payback Coupon Book, was founded in 2001 on a simple three part philosophy:

  • To help local schools and non profit groups raise much needed money with an easy and profitable fundraiser.
  • To offer area merchants a LOCAL, "neighborhood marketing" strategy that also helps in their goal of supporting their community.
  • To save families money CLOSE TO HOME while eating out, shopping, going to movies, visiting local attractions and more.

Payback's founder, Ted Shamp, spent 12 years with Entertainment Publications, (publishers of the Entertainment and Gold C coupon books) most recently as Regional Manager -- Fundraising Division for Oregon/Idaho/Hawaii.

Upon leaving his position with Entertainment, Ted saw the need for a discount product that focused more on bringing value to the smaller, growing communities that most other companies ignored. The first Payback Book launched in Vancouver, Wash in 2001, followed by Boulder County, Colorado in 2009 and Bend/Central Oregon in 2014.

Payback has succeeded and grown by truly "paying back" the schools, non-profits and families in its markets. Schools and groups receive a generous 50% profit on all Payback Book/Mobile Apps they sell. Merchants benefit through the extensive "word of mouth" network that Payback helps foster through its introductory discounts. Local families benefit by discovering new merchants NEAR home, and becoming loyal, regular customers.

Neighbor marketing at its simplest - and best...
